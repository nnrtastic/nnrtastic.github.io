WELCOME TO OUR RESTAURANT
───────

Since 2021, our restaurant has been a go-to destination for authentic Vietnamese food in Launceston. Conveniently located in the CH Building, next to Service Tasmania, we are proud to offer a variety of traditional dishes that locals love.

From our freshly made Bánh Mì and fragrant Phở to our Gỏi Cuốn and crispy Spring Rolls, every dish is prepared with the finest ingredients to deliver the true flavors of Vietnam. Visit us today and discover why we're known as the best Vietnamese restaurant in town!
