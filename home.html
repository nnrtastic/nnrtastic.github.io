Welcome to our restaurant

Established in 2021, we are a favorite among many locals. Conveniently located in the CH Building, right next to Service Tasmania, our restaurant is known for serving up some of the best Vietnamese dishes in town.

Our Bánh Mì, Phở, Gỏi Cuốn, and Spring Rolls have earned the love and loyalty of our customers. Come visit us and experience the flavors that keep people coming back!
